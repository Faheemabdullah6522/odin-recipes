Aloo Paratha

Description

Aloo paratha is a popular Indian stuffed flatbread filled with spiced mashed potatoes. It is crisp outside, soft inside, and usually enjoyed hot with yogurt, pickle, or butter, especially as a hearty breakfast.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups wheat flour (for dough)
  • 2 medium potatoes (boiled and mashed)
  • 1 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • Salt to taste
  • Chopped coriander (optional)
  • Oil or ghee for cooking

Steps

  1. Make dough using wheat flour, water, and a pinch of salt; rest it.
  2. Mix mashed potatoes with chili powder, cumin, salt, and coriander.
  3. Roll dough into small circles, stuff with potato mix, and seal.
  4. Roll out again gently and cook on a hot tawa with oil or ghee.
  5. Serve hot with curd or pickle.
