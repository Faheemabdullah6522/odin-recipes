Suji Halwa

Description

Suji halwa is a quick Indian dessert made from roasted semolina (suji), ghee, sugar, and water. It's often prepared during festivals or as a simple sweet treat and is loved for its soft, melt-in-mouth texture and rich flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup suji (semolina)
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup ghee
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • A few cardamom pods
  • Chopped nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat ghee in a pan, add suji, and roast until golden and aromatic.
  2. In another pot, boil water with sugar and cardamom.
  3. Slowly pour sugar water into the roasted suji (carefully, it may splutter).
  4. Stir continuously until it thickens and ghee separates.
  5. Garnish with nuts and serve warm.
